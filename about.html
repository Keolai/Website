Keolailani "Keolai" Rose is a young painter and digital artist from San Antonio, Texas, who focuses on the field of surrealism. She draws inspiration from all parts of her life, from ordinary objects, to music, to even the lives and emotions of those close to her. Although her artwork may be seen as dark or sinister in nature, those around her would describe her as relatively humorous, kind and even somewhat shy. Apart from painting, she enjoys vintage tech, goth rock, and the DOOM video game series.
